Pork Shoulder


Description

Smoked Pork Shoulder is a BBQ favorite. It can be served on its own individually or pulled and put on a sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 8 lb. pork shoulder butt
  • 1/4 cup Famous Dave's Rib Rub
  • Enough smoking wood to run your smoker at 275 degrees F for ~12 hours

Steps

  1. Rub the Rib Rub onto the pork shoulder butt
  2. Smoke at 275 degrees F until internal temperature reads 202 degrees F
