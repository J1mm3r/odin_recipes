Texas Style Brisket


Description

Texas Style Brisket is steeped in Czech and German traditions brought to the United States. Salt, pepper, and smoke are all that's required.

Ingredients

  • Full packer brisket (10-17 lbs.)
  • 1/4 cup coarse salt
  • 1/4 cup coarse black pepper
  • Enough smoking wood to run your smoker at 275 degrees F for ~12 hours

Steps

  1. Combine salt and pepper in shaker
  2. Apply salt and pepper liberally to all sides of the brisket
  3. Smoke at 275 degrees F until internal temperature reads 202 degrees F
